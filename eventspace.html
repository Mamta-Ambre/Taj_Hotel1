- EVENT SPACES -


-Hotels usually have plenty of spaces to host various types of events. The events being hosted are not only a good source of income for hotels, but events also add to the popularity and branding efforts of a hotel.

-Events organized at hotels are a source of gaiety and joy for people that use a hotel as a venue for their special event gatherings. The ambiance and the amazing food served by a hotel according to the event and the customer’s requirements result in an occasion that’s memorable for all the attendees.

-This blog post will discuss some of the events that you can arrange in a top-class hotel in the Mornington Peninsula.



WEDDINGS

1. A wedding is a type of event that is organized in all hotels that host event gatherings. If you look for the Mornington Peninsula events, you’ll find that weddings are the red-hot occasion that are arranged in different hotels with a lot of nice-looking arrangements.

2. For the reason of specialness and a sense of memorable gathering, weddings are close to the heart of the celebrants or attendees. This is the main reason why people do a lot of research and effort to make sure the venue is impressive.

3. Keeping these needs and desires under consideration, hotels arrange facilities and amenities that befit events like wedding ceremonies.



CONFERENCE MEETINGS

1. Most hotels have rooms for the purpose of arranging events like conference meetings. From large-sized rooms to big halls accommodating hundreds of people are available at the hotel premises for arranging a business gathering.

2. Conference meetings hold a special significance in terms of their business value. Be it a meeting with the investors, the stakeholders of a project, an annual dinner, or a small conference meeting – all such gatherings have a formal touch to them.

3. Many business organizations and associations prefer hotels for arranging conference meetings and other business gatherings. Hotels have the space and facilities to accommodate and arrange for large and small business gatherings. The great thing about hotels as venues for conference meetings is that a hotel can serve as both a venue and a place of accommodation for staying.

4. Before or after the meeting, all the guests can go to their rooms and spend time in comfort.



THE PRODUCT LAUNCH

1. A purely professional event, the product launch (or kick-off for intimates) is in charge of promoting a new product. After months, even years of work, the long-awaited moment has finally arrived! You plan to launch your new product or service on the market and to do so, you gather:

2. Often highlighted, this event usually takes place in the evening, in a prestigious location. As we have said, the audience is often made up of journalists, so we have to be careful not to overdo it! This target is particularly demanding and difficult to seduce, which is why product launch organizers rely on originality and surprise.

3. Steve Jobs, founder of Apple, is the one who has revolutionized product launches in recent years by organizing grandiose events that attract professionals and enthusiasts as well as the curious.



THE TEAM BUILDING

1.This is an event that organizers often like to be entrusted with: team building. Its purpose is simple: to give a good time to your team of collaborators by reinforcing the cohesion between them and, above all, by motivating them. You have to keep in mind that the objectives can vary according to the clients. For example, it could be the resolution of internal problems through creative and enriching activities, or the improvement of a team’s performance through collaborative and unifying activities.

2.Team building is often organized at the request of the director or team leader. It is held over one or two days, but rarely extends over a longer period.

3.In short, it is a fun event that combines work and play activities. And that is the magic of team building! Mixing original activities, which allow learning while having fun, while fostering the spirit of competition and teamwork.

4.Also, team building is a way of rewarding employees other than by giving them a bonus. And yes, between treasure hunts, murals, escape games and virtual reality activities… There’s plenty to motivate the troops by giving a bit of pep to their daily work.



WORKSHOPS

1. As hotels have various spaces for meetings and gatherings, events like workshops can easily be accommodated on the hotel premises.

2. From setting up a stage to the arrangement of quality food and sound systems, the hotel’s service will accommodate everything so you have the ideal workshop. No matter what the workshop is about, the hotel management knows how to arrange for all that you can ask for..



OUTDOOR CINEMA AND FILM FESTIVALS

1. Outdoor cinemas and film festivals are now an ongoing trend. And all such events are mainly held in hotels due to the ambiance, the feel, the food, and the overall services the hotel management can provide.

2. The whole environment in which people watch their favorite movie at a hotel under the open skies is an experience one instinctively craves.

.



SOCIAL GATHERING(BIRTHDAY, ANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION)

1.These are small events that last for a few hours. All the requirements are told by the customers including what will be the menu for the event.

2.It can be a small kitty party of 10 people or any kind of celebration for 50 people.
