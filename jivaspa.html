4. CHAMPI

Since centuries, this popular Indian head massage is a lavish treatment that promotes hair growth and restores the hair’s natural glossiness. For the massage, we use our signature blend of time honoured ingredients such as Amla, Brahmi, Jatamansi, Curry leaves and Neem, which are known for their enriching attributes. The treatment helps in discharging muscular tension from the head, neck and shoulders to help you relax and clear your mind. All while adding a natural sheen to your hair.
